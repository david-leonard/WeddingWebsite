OUR STORY

Just your classic story of a small town girl and a city boy - Sarah moved to London from Chatham and David, from Thornhill. They first crossed paths in 2011 when they worked together at Fanshawe College's on-campus restaurant, Oasis. David as the stir-fry guy, Sarah, the cashier, he would write notes to her on people's take-out boxes to "break the communication barrier". They started as friends, with just a little, light flirting. And that's how it stayed, for a while. It wasn't until a few months after graduation, they ran into eachother on the city bus, caught up while travelling down Richmond Street and exchanged numbers. David asked Sarah if she would be his girlfriend "for a bit" in February 2013, she happily said yes!
